What is the image quality of the converted JPG files?

Pages are rendered at 2× resolution (approximately 150–200 DPI) providing sharp, high-quality images suitable for web, email, and presentation use. PNG output is lossless for maximum quality.

Can I convert a multi-page PDF to multiple JPG images?

Yes. All pages are converted simultaneously. For multi-page PDFs, the images are bundled into a ZIP archive so you can download all pages with one click.

What is the difference between PDF to JPG and PDF to PNG?

JPG (JPEG) produces smaller file sizes and is ideal for photographs and complex images. PNG is lossless, supports transparency, and is better for text-heavy documents where maximum sharpness matters.

Can I extract images from a PDF?

Yes. Converting PDF to JPG effectively extracts each page as an image. If you want specific embedded images, the PDF to Image tool renders each page at high resolution capturing all visual content.
